TAROT CARD READINGS

What is tarot? According to Merriam-Webster tarot is: any of a set of 78 playing cards including 22 pictorial cards used for fortune-telling.

While this is the most basic definition out there, tarot to me is so much more. Reading tarot cards is a form of magic, with a little psychology sprinkled in. When I read, I tell clients that I am a "Spiritual Therapist" and am here to help them work on things they may not have even known they needed work on. I look at the imagery of the card, how the card came out of the deck (upright or upside down), the other cards in the reading, where it lays in the line up of cards. All of these things help piece together the puzzle of the issue.

HOME CLEANSING & BLESSING

Do you feel a lot of stagnant energy in your home? Have someone over who carries a dark cloud with them and now the house energy feels "off"?

I offer Home Cleansing and Blessing services! Home Cleansing is a good spiritual scrubbing of the energy of a space, via many different methods. I personally use herb smoke to rid a space of "negative" (stagnant or funky) energy.

Once the energy is removed, it creates a void or open space in the home, which is where "Blessing" comes into play. To bless a home, I gather herbs that are needed to give the home the new spiritual energy the client desires (for example, and herbal mix for bringing in "love, peace, and happiness" would have it's own herbal mix). I then burn these in a burn safe container and want the smoke throughout the space that was just cleansed.

Prices for this will vary depending on size of space.
