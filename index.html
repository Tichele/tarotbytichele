TAROT CARD READINGS

What is tarot? According to Merriam-Webster tarot is: any of a set of 78 playing cards including 22 pictorial cards used for fortune-telling.

While this is the most basic definition out there, tarot to me is so much more. Reading tarot cards is a form of magic, with a little psychology sprinkled in. When I read I tell clients that I am a "Spiritual Therapist" and am here to help them work on things they may not have even known they needed work on.
