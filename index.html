HOME CLEANSING & BLESSING

Do you feel a lot of stagnant energy in your home? Have someone over who carries a dark cloud with them and now the house energy feels "off"?

I offer Home Cleansing and Blessing services!

Home Cleansing is a good spiritual scrubbing of the energy of a space, via many different methods. I personally use herb smoke to rid a space of "negative" (stagnant or funky) energy.

Once the energy is removed, it creates a void or open space in the home, which is where "Blessing" comes into play.

To bless a home, I gather herbs that are needed to give the home the new spiritual energy the client desires (for example, and herbal mix for bringing in "love, peace, and happiness" would have it's own herbal mix). I then burn these in a burn safe container and waft the smoke throughout the space that was just cleansed.

Prices for this will vary depending on size of space.
